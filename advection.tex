\subsubsection*{Problem 3} I solved the advection equation $u_t + u_x = 0$ using four different numerical methods: 2nd order centered difference, 4th order centered difference, 6th order centered difference, and spectral collocation method. For time-stepping, I used a 6th order Runge-Kutta scheme. The equation was solved on an interval of length $2 \pi$, and periodic boundary conditions were used. I used grid sizes of 8, 16, 32, 64, 128, and 256 points. The time step for all cases was 0.003, which is small enough to ensure that the CFL number for all grid sizes is less than 1. \\
